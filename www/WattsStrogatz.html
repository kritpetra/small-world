Watts-Strogatz Model

A large residential block has just finished construction. One person each moves into a house, which together spans the entire circumference of the block. On the first day, everybody introduces themselves to their closest neighbors. Then, over the next few weeks, random acquaintances are made as residents go about their day, while a similar number of neighbors fall out of contact.
